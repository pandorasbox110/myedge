Different Kinds of Nouns

Common nouns are subdivided into concrete, abstract, mass, and count nouns.

A concrete noun names something that can be perceived through the senses - song, bridge, priest, school, etc.

An abstract noun names a quality, a thought, or an idea - talent, belief, friendship, independence, etc.
