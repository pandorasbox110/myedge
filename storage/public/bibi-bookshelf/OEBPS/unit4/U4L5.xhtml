Subtraction of 2 -Digit Numbers



Let Us Learn

Read the story.

Father bought 28 roses. He gave 18 roses to Mother. How many roses does Father still have?

To answer the question, write the subtraction sentence as shown below.


28
− 18

Subtract the digits in the ones place.

Tens Ones
2 8
− 1 8
0

Then subtract the digits in the tens place.

Tens Ones
2 8
− 1 8
1 0

The difference is 10. So Father still has 10 roses.
