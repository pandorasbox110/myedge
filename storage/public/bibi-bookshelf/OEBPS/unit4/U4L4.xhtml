Subtraction of a l -Digit Number from a 2 -Digit Number



Let Us Learn

Read the story.

Mr. Reyes has 75 cans of milk in his store. He sold 3 cans of milk yesterday. How many cans of milk does he still have in his store?

To answer the question, write the minuend and the subtrahend as shown below.


75
− 3

Subtract the digits in the ones place.

There is only one number in the tens place. Bring down 7.

Thus, 75 − 3 = 72 . Mr. Reyes still has 72 cans of milk in his store.
