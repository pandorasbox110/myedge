Subtraction Using a Number Line



Let Us Learn

Look at the number line. Read the sentences that follow.

Niño jumped forward from 0 to 6. Then he jumped backward 4 times.

How many jumps did Niño make to move forward?
How many jumps did he make to move backward?
How many jumps did he not make to return to 0?


A number line may also be used in subtracting numbers.


The given word problem is shown by the subtraction sentence below.

6 − 4 = 2

The subtraction sentence shows that:

  • 6 is the number of jumps Niño made from 0 to 6.
  • 4 is the number of jumps Niño made backward from 6 to 2.
  • 2 is the number of jumps Niño did not make backward to return to 0.
