Subtraction from l0 or Less



Let Us Learn

Count all the flowers in the set. Then count the flowers that were crossed out. How many flowers are left in the set?

Taking away 3 flowers from the set of 8 is subtracting them. Subtraction is taking away something from a whole or from a set. The subtraction sentence for the set of flowers is:

The subtraction sentence is read as “eight minus three is equal to five.”

The minuend is 8. It is the number from which a number is subtracted.

The minus sign (−) is the symbol used in subtraction.

The subtrahend is 3. It is the number subtracted or taken away from the minuend.

The equal sign (=) is used to show the answer.

The difference is the answer in subtraction.
