Time by the Hour



Let Us Learn

Look at the clock. A clock tells time. It has the numbers 1 to 12.

A clock has two hands: the hour hand and the minute hand.

The short hand is the hour hand. It points to the hour on the clock.

The long hand is the minute hand. It points to the minute on the clock.

Look at the example below. When the hour hand is pointing at 6 and the long hand is pointing at 12, the time is 6 o’clock.

The time can be written as 6:00.
