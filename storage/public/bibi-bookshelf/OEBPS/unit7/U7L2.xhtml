Time by the Half Hour



Let Us Learn

Listen to your teacher read the story.

Joneil hears his alarm clock ringing. It is time for him to wake up. He looks at his alarm clock.

Look at the picture above. The time on the clock shows 5:30.

There are 60 minutes in 1 hour. A half hour is 30 minutes.

The short hand points between 5 and 6 and the long hand points to 6. You read it as “five thirty.” You can also read it as “half past 5” or “thirty minutes after five o’clock.”


Read the time shown in each clock.
