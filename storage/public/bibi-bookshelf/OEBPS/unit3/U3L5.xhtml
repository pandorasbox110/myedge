Addition of 2 -Digit Numbers



Let Us Learn

In adding 2-digit numbers, write first the addends in columns.

The answer to 45 + 2 is 47.


Now, add these 2-digit numbers: 23 and 46.

The sum of 23 and 46 is 69.
