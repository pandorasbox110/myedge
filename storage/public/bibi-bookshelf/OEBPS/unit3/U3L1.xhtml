Addition with Sums Up to l0



Let Us Learn

Look at the balls in box A and in box B. Putting together the balls from these two boxes means adding the balls. This is called addition.

Addition is putting two or more numbers together. The addition sentence for the sets of balls is shown below.

The addition sentence is read as “five plus four is equal to nine.”
The addends are 5 and 4. These are the numbers that you add.
The plus sign (+) is the symbol used in addition.
The equal sign (=) is used to show the answer.
The sum is the answer in addition.
