Word Problems on Addition



Let Us Learn

A word problem is an exercise that is in the form of a story. In solving a word problem, you must follow these steps:

Step 1. Know what is asked for.
Step 2. Know what are given.
Step 3. Write the addition sentence.
Step 4. Write the complete answer.

Below is an example of a word problem. See how it is solved using the given steps.

There are 4 boys and 3 girls playing in the playground. How many children are there in all?

Step 1. What is asked? total number of children
Step 2. What are given? 4 boys and 3 girls
Step 3. What is the addition sentence? 4 + 3 = 7
Step 4. What is the answer? There are 7 children in all.
