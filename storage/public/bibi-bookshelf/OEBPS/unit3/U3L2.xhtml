Freddie the Frog

Freddie the frog wants to go to the pond.
He makes 5 jumps from numbers 0 to 5.
Then he makes 5 jumps more from 5 to l 0.

How many jumps did Freddie the frog make to get to the pond?

A number line is also used to add numbers. Here is an example to show it.

The number line shows the addition sentence 4 + 5 = 9 . The boy jumped over 4 numbers first. Then he jumped over 5 numbers. He jumped over 9 numbers in all.
