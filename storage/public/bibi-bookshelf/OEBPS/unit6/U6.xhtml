Days and Months



Days
(Tune of “Jack and Jill”)

Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday Thursday, Friday, Saturday,
These are all the days in a week.
Let’s play and dance in laughter.


Months
(Tune of “The Alphabet Song”)

January, February, March,
April, May, June, and July,
August, September, October,
November, and December,
Now I know the months in a year.
Every month gives me great cheer.
