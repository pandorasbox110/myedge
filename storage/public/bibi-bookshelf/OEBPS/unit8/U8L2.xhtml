Word Problems About Money



Let Us Learn

Read the word problem with your teacher.

Carl bought a toy car for ₱50. He also bought batteries worth ₱36.

How much did Carl pay in all?

Say again the four steps you learned in solving a word problem.

Step 1.
Know what is asked for.
Step 2.
Know what are given.
Step 3.
Write the number sentence.
Step 4.
Write the complete answer.

Solve the problem above.

Step 1.
What is asked? amount of money paid in all
Step 2.
What are given? ₱50, price of the toy car; ₱36, price of the batteries
Step 3.
What is the number sentence?
₱50 + ₱36 = ₱86
or
₱50
+ 36
₱86
Step 4.
What is the answer? Carl paid ₱86 in all.
