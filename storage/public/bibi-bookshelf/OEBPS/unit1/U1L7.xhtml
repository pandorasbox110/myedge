Greater Than , Less Than , or Equal to



Let Us Learn

You can also compare numbers using the words greater than, less than, or equal to. Listen to your teacher read each sentence. Repeat after him or her.

Use the symbol > if the first number is greater than the second number.

Example: 25 > 20

Use the symbol < if the first number is less than the second number.

Example: 12 < 21

Use the symbol = if the numbers are the same or have the same value.

Example: 100 = 100


Let Us Try
