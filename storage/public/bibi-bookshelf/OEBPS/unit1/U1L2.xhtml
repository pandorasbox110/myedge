Tens and Ones



Let Us Learn

Numbers from l0 to 99 have tens and ones. Study the examples below.

There are 16 cupcakes. The number l6 has l ten and 6 ones.

1 ten and 6 ones make 16.

There are 24 strawberries. The number 24 stands for 2 tens and 4 ones.

2 tens and 4 ones make 24.

There are 35 candies. The number 35 means there are 3 tens and 5 ones.

3 tens and 5 ones make 35.
