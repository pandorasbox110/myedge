One Part of a Whole



Let Us Learn

Look at the picture of a pizza. How many slices does the pizza have? Each slice of the pizza is represented by the fraction 1 2 .

A fraction names a part of a whole. It has a numerator, a denominator, and a fraction bar.

The numerator tells how many equal parts are taken from the whole. The numerator in the fraction 1 2 is 1.

The denominator tells how many equal parts the whole or a set is divided into. The denominator in the fraction 1 2 is 2.

The line that separates the numerator from the denominator is called the fraction bar.


Here are some examples of fraction.

One of two equal parts is one-half.

One of three equal parts is one-third.

One of four equal parts is one-fourth.

One of five equal parts is one-fifth.

One of six equal parts is one-sixth.
