Two or More Parts of a Whole



Let Us Learn

Two of three equal parts is two-thirds.

Three of four equal parts is three-fourths.

Four of six equal parts is four-sixths.

Two of five equal parts is two-fifths.

Three of eight equal parts is three-eighths.

Four of seven equal parts is four-sevenths.
