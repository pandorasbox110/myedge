Odd and Even Numbers



Let Us Learn

Numbers may be odd or even.

The numbers l, 3, 5, 7, and 9 are odd numbers.

Numbers that end in l, 3, 5, 7, and 9 are also odd numbers. Look at the other examples of odd numbers below.

The numbers 0, 2, 4, 6, and 8 are even numbers.

Numbers that end in 0, 2, 4, 6, and 8 are also even numbers. Look at the other examples of even numbers.
