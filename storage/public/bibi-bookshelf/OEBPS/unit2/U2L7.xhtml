Skip Counting by 2 s



Let Us Learn

Skip counting means counting by numbers other than 1. Look at the number lines. Listen to your teacher skip count by 2s. Repeat after him or her.


Let Us Try

A.
Skip count by 2s. Write the missing number in each box to complete the chart.

B.
Skip count by 2s. Write the missing numbers in each set.

Let Us Practice

Count by 2s. Write each missing number in the blank.
