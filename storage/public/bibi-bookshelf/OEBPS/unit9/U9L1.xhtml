Length



Let Us Learn

Look at the shoelace and the belt. How long are they?
You can measure their length by using your pencil.

The shoelace is 3 pencils long.
The belt is 4 pencils long.

You can also measure the length of other things using shorter things. For example, you may measure the length of your pencil case using paper clips.

The pencil case is 6 paper clips long.
