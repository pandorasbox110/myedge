Height



Let Us Learn

Look at the picture of the boy and the girl. Tell how tall they are by counting the number of straws.

The boy is 5 drinking straws tall. The girl is 4 drinking straws tall.

You can also measure the height of other things by using shorter things. For example, you may measure the height of your desk using your pencils.


Let Us Try

Look at each thing. Find its height by counting the number of matchsticks beside it. Write your answer on the line.

vase = _____ matchsticks
glass = _____ matchsticks
mug = _____ matchsticks

Let Us Practice

A.
Pair up with a classmate. Measure the height of each thing by using crayons. Write the answer on the line.
  1. your teacher’s table = _____ crayons
  2. your desk = _____ crayons
  3. your teacher’s chair = _____ crayons
  4. your chair = _____ crayons
  5. your longest pencil = _____ crayons
