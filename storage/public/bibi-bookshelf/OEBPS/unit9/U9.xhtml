Measurements and Shapes



When We Measure
(Tune of “Where Is Thumbman?”)

When we measure, when we measure
Length of things, height of things
Measure length from left to right,
Measure height from up to down,
Left to right, up to down.

Look at the shapes, there are stars,
Triangles, diamonds,
Circles, rectangles, squares,
Pentagons, hexagons,
Heptagons to decagons.
